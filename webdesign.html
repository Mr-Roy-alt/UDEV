Web Research & Design Services

Our Procedure

WEB RESEACH PHASE

We'll start with an email to gain a better understanding of what you're searching for, whether you're in India, Cameroon, or elsewhere. We'll talk about web design, features, and functioning as we go over your fantastic ideas. If you have any concerns about your future website, we'll make sure to address them as well. This section will give you a better idea of what to expect during our website design and development process.

UX DESIGN PHASE

One of our online professional team will review your website, web design, or web application's user experience and flows. You'll learn how to become a web ninja here. You'll understand where each button takes you, what each call to action includes, and how each one transforms into a potential sale. We'll sketch out your site map so you can see how a user will traverse the site and engage with your future web design.

UI DESIGN PHASE

This is when we "dress" the user flows into a real web design, which will subsequently become your website. Now that we've described the user path and flows, it's time to put the finishing touches on your website. Consider branding, colors, palettes, and aesthetics, keeping in mind user friendliness and ease of use. If you have brand rules for site design, colors, logos, and other things, we'll follow them exactly. Don't worry if you haven't done so yet; our team of Web Design specialists is ready to help.

  • 1
    SKETCHING

    Drawing is a kind of brainstorming. UI/UX designers generate lots of concepts, sketch them out, then pick the finest ones. Instead of creating a full interface for each idea, they sketch out the visual concept of the software application.

  • 2
    WIREFRAMING

    Wireframing is the most significant part of the UI/UX software design process for visualizing future deliverables. Corrections are made during this stage by developers. Establishing a solution for user task implementation is critical.

  • 2
    VISUALIZATION (MOCKUPS)

    This stage involves visualizing and branding the interface elements of a software product. Your application now has a distinct visual identity. Based on the approved UI/UX design and project documents, the software development team provides a complete estimate for the development step.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

It's coding time after we obtain your approval! We'll turn your approved web design into a fully functional website, web application, or mobile app. We'll go through a digital sprint to guarantee your website is constructed and ready to conquer the web, keeping SEO, accessibility, and web standards in mind. This is where your product will come to life in a matter of weeks, not years.

THINK OUTSIDE THE BOX

You'll get a website that's lightning fast (Google tested) and follows the most recent web development standards. We'll create a multi-device friendly website that's responsive on mobile, tablet, desktop, and all of your other platforms, as well as exports to your project's web design in Figma or Adobe. You'll get a custom CMS (Content Management System) that's easy to use and customised to your requirements.

QUALITY ASSURANCE TESTING

You've got your web design turned into a ready-to-go website or web app, but now it's time to put it through a thorough QA test to ensure that all of the website's links work, that any bugs have been fixed, and that any final polishes have been applied before your website waves hello to the digital universe in Cyprus, India, London, or anywhere else in the world.
